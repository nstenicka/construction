Aberfeldy Home Construction

Our mission at Aberfeldy Home Construction is the creation and construction of exciting homes featuring best in class floor plans, constructed to the best industry practices creating a safe peaceful sanctuary for our customers. We acheive this by incorporating the latest technology, building methods and materials. John learned how to build in the mountains of Colorado with homes at 8300-9100 feet above sea level and is so excited to be able to design and build homes for the beautiful Gulf Coast.

What Aberfeldy means..

To John, Aberfeldy is a link between the old and the new. Aberfeldy is a burgh in Perth and Kinross, Scotland, on the River Tay. A small market town. It holds a lot of history to the family. To John's mother, in wartime Scotland as a young lass, Aberfeldy was to be a secure, safe and peaceful place away from the potential dangers and hardships of 1940.
"Aberfeldy…While I was a young school girl, my sister and I were evacuated to a safe location away from the suspected landing zone of the fierce German paratroopers in 1940-Aberfeldy. While just a single week of my life, I hold strong clear family memories from Aberfeldy. As an 11 year old, I waved and kissed my father for the last time and was the end of my childhood…Some 50 years later, I returned with my sister for a vacation together. The town, like the two of us, had changed in many ways. Aberfeldy was to be our last vacation together in our beloved Scotland.” -Kathleen Stenicka The homes we design and build at Aberfeldy Home Construction are secure, safe, warm sanctuaries for families to enjoy and add memories here on Florida's beautiful Gulf Coast.
